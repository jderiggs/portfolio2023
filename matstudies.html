A suite of procedurally generated parameter-driven materials created using Adobe Substance 3D Designer and rendered in Marmoset Toolbag 4. They can be used in any 3D design workflow, from rendering to game design, and are created by me for practice and the texturing of models for Product Design or Game Design. These textures take into account the physical properties of the material such as roughness, metallic and height. This page will serve as a repository for materials that I’ve created for this purpose.


Project Description

“
Exploration and showcase of 3D Materials for the express use in Product Design, Rendering and Game Design.
”
